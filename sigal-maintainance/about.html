About Sigal

Founded in 1995, Sigal World for Medical Services is a leading company in providing elite dental services in Saudi Arabia for more than 25 years by utilizing best practices and acquiring talented dental consultant pioneers and experienced technical team in the dental industry.
One of the major keys for this excellent reputation in the field, is the use of top ranked dental equipment, consumables, and materials that’s reflected throughout many years of quality-based services which is a part of the company's strategy.

Aligned with Saudi’s MOH VRO programs, Sigal adopted the patient-centered dental care module for years. Out of this perspective and by having great business mindset, experience, passion, and ambition, Sigal became a closed joint stock company and expanded its exceptional services to increase accessibility to quality-based dental services in the kingdom.

Our Mission

Elevating patient's confidence by expressing prominent esthetic smile with competent durability and functionality.

Our Vission

To enable dental practitioners, Laboratories, and dental facilities to deliver best practicing treatment methods to serve their patients’ needs by utilizing up to date finest, innovative, and affordable solutions and services in the region, assuring and maintaining competency and profitability of their practice.
