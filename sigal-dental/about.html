About Us

Sigal is the Private Dental clinic with more than 25 years’ experience in serving patients. Sigal was established by a group of Dental Consultants and dedicated in providing our patients with the highest quality of dental care in all dental specialties. Sigal Dental Clinic has been set up two successful practices in the Riyadh. Sigal Dental Clinic – Olaya and Sigal Dental Clinic Takassusi

Founded in 1995, The Sigal Dental clinic team has established itself as the leader in the field of Dental treatments. Our excellent reputation has been earned by the consistent high quality and outstanding results provided by our highly qualified and experienced Dental and Technical team.
